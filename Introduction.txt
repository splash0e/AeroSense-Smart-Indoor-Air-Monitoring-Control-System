Indoor air quality and environmental conditions have a direct impact on comfort, health, and productivity, yet they are often ignored in everyday living spaces. 
This project, AeroSense, is designed to address that gap by creating a simple and affordable system that continuously monitorstemperature, humidity, and air 
quality in real time.
The system not only displays environmental data but also responds to it intelligently. When conditions go beyond a defined comfort range, it 
automatically activates a filtration mechanism to improve air quality. By combining basic sensors, automation, and wireless control, this prototype
demonstrates a practical step toward smarter and more energy-efficient indoor environments.
