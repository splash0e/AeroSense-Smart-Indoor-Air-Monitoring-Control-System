1. Basic Structure - 
| Temperature | Humidity | Air Quality | Condition Status   | Motor (Filter) |
| ----------- | -------- | ----------- | ------------------ | -------------- |
| Normal      | Normal   | Good        | Comfortable        | OFF            |
| High        | Normal   | Good        | High Temperature   | ON             |
| Normal      | High     | Good        | High Humidity      | ON             |
| Normal      | Normal   | Poor        | Poor Air Quality   | ON             |
| High        | High     | Poor        | Critical Condition | ON             |
| Normal      | Normal   | Normal      | Stable Environment | OFF            |

2. Working - 
The system continuously monitors environmental conditions using the DHT11 and air quality sensor. The Arduino processes 
these inputs in real time and compares them with predefined threshold values for temperature, humidity, and air quality.
If any parameter exceeds its safe limit, the Arduino sends a signal to the relay module, which activates the 
motor (filter/fan). This helps bring the environment back to a comfortable range. Meanwhile, all sensor readings 
are displayed on the LCD for real-time monitoring. Once the conditions return to normal, the system 
automatically turns the motor OFF, ensuring energy-efficient operation
